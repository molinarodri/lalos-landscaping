Services

Lalo's Landscaping is dedicated to enhancing, transforming, and/or maintaining your property. We work with our clients to achieve their desired results in the following areas: Portland, Beaverton, Hillsboro, Damascus, Oregon City, Fairview, Vancouver, Lake Oswego, Happy Valley, Estacada. Lalo's Landscaping offers a variety of services including, but not limited to:

  • Lawn care
  • Weeding
  • Shrubs
  • Fences
  • Removing unwanted, dead, or damaged trees
  • Planting
  • Pressure Washer
  • Installing sod in the garden
  • Gravel driveways
  • Custom driveway for garden
  • Mowing, mulching landscape beds, edging, pruning
